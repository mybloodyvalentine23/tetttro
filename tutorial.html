How to Play Tetris

Welcome to Tetris! The goal is to clear lines by filling them with falling blocks.

  • Controls: Use arrow keys to move and rotate blocks.
  • Objective: Clear horizontal lines to score points.
  • Tips: Plan ahead and rotate blocks to fit gaps.
  • Game Over: When blocks reach the top, the game ends.

Have fun and aim for high scores!
